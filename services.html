Service From Begining To End

At Bamboo, we believe that your project should be kept as simple and straight forward as possible, regardless of how complex the task may seem. Wherever possible, we try to use local established and trusted subcontractors and suppliers. We undertake a broad range of projects ranging across the following activities:

  • Residential
  • Commercail
  • New Build
  • Refurbishment
  • Design & Build
  • Interiors & Fit Out
  • Surveying

Design & Build

Bamboo offers a complete package to their clients where they provide a full design & build and Project Management service for any project, providing a single point of contact from conception to completion. Bamboo take on all the risk by committing to complete the project for a fixed contract sum, within a fixed duration to an agreed specification and design. Bamboo will manage every aspect of the project and ensure all works are completed on time and to our usual high standard, and that everything is in place and working so our clients can simply walk in and start living in thier dream home, or trading in thier new business premises.

Commercial

Bamboo work with their clients to locate and create interiors that not only compliment the business’ that occupy them but create an environment people want to work and socialize in. We use our extensive experience, flair and innovation to design, plan and build an environment that combines practicality with style. We work hard to help our clients find the most suitable properties and ensure they provide sufficient space and services for both the present and future occupation.

Retail

Bamboo has a bespoke service for retail companies by locating, securing and refurbishing properties. Synergy operates throughout the Channel Islands working with both national and local independent retailers to ensure they maximize their presence whilst making best use of the sales floor and always keeping an eye on costs.
